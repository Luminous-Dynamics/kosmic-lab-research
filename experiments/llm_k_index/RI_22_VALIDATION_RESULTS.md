**Option C: Weight Layers Differently**
- Top discriminators (causal, variability, creativity) get higher weight
- Consistency gets lower weight (counterintuitive in current form)
- Would increase separation between mimicry and consciousness

**Recommended: Combination of B + C**
- Refine consistency to catch contradictions not vocabulary variation
- Weight layers by discriminative power (causal, variability, creativity higher)
