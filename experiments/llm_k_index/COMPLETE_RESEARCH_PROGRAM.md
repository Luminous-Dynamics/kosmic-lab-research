## 🗺️ Track 2: Full 7+1D Testing on LLMs

### Goal
Compute complete Kosmic K-Vector for humans, small LLMs, and frontier models

### Timeline: Week 2-4

### Phase 2.1: Adapt Dimensions for Conversations (Week 2)

**K_R (Reactivity)**: ✅ Already working
- Correlation between conversation turn embeddings

**K_A (Agency)**:
- Definition: Do LLM responses systematically change the conversation trajectory?
- Measure: ρ(||response_t||, Δ||conversation_{t+1}||)
- Hypothesis: Humans have higher K_A (more influence on conversation direction)

**K_I (Integration)**:
- Definition: Does response complexity match conversation complexity?
- Measure: Entropy matching between turn lengths/semantics
- Hypothesis: LLMs may have lower K_I (formulaic responses)

**K_P (Prediction)**:
- Definition: Can next turn be predicted from history?
- Measure: Prediction error using world model
- Hypothesis: Humans may have higher predictability (coherent narratives)

**K_M (Meta/Temporal)**:
- Definition: Does conversation use its own history?
- Measure: History-aware vs Markov prediction improvement
- Hypothesis: GPT-4o should have high K_M (long context)

**K_S (Social)**:
- Definition: Coherence between conversation participants
- Measure: Turn-level coordination
- Hypothesis: Humans have higher K_S (mutual modeling)

**K_H (Harmonic)**:
- Definition: Conversation efficiency (information per turn)
- Measure: Information density / turn length
- Hypothesis: Unclear - may vary by domain

**K_Topo (Operational Closure)**: ✅ Already validated
- Persistent homology of semantic trajectories
