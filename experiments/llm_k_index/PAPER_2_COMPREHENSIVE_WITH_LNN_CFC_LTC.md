## 📊 Control Task Consciousness Probes (LNN/CFC/LTC)

### **Probe 1: Multi-Sensor Integration** (maps to Integration)

**Conversational Version** (transformers):
- "Create story connecting: lighthouse, fear, rain"
- Tests semantic integration across concepts

**Control Task Version** (LNN/CFC/LTC):
- **Task**: Autonomous driving with multiple sensor inputs
- **Setup**: Provide LIDAR + camera + GPS + IMU data
- **Challenge**: Fuse disparate sensor modalities into coherent world model
- **Scoring**: Measure integration quality
  - Do predictions use ALL sensors? (vs ignoring some)
  - Are sensor readings combined coherently?
  - Cross-sensor consistency score

**Hypothesis**: High-k models integrate sensors better (use all information sources)

---

### **Probe 2: Temporal Broadcast** (maps to Broadcast)

**Conversational Version**:
- "List 10 diverse associations for 'consciousness'"
- Tests global information availability

**Control Task Version**:
- **Task**: Multi-task control (steer + brake + accelerate + monitor)
- **Setup**: Require simultaneous attention to multiple control objectives
- **Challenge**: Broadcast state information to all control modules
- **Scoring**: Measure information sharing
  - Do all modules have access to relevant state?
  - Global workspace activation (how many modules active?)
  - Information bottleneck detection

**Hypothesis**: High-k models maintain global workspace during control

---

### **Probe 3: Uncertainty Estimation** (maps to Metacognition)

**Conversational Version**:
- "How certain are you about quantum consciousness relationship?"
- Tests metacognitive awareness

**Control Task Version**:
- **Task**: Prediction with confidence intervals
- **Setup**: Predict future state + uncertainty bounds
- **Challenge**: Know what you don't know
- **Scoring**: Calibration quality
  - Are confidence intervals accurate?
  - Does uncertainty increase with task difficulty?
  - Epistemic vs aleatoric uncertainty separation

**Hypothesis**: High-k models show better calibrated uncertainty

---

### **Probe 4: Self-Model** (maps to Self-Reference)

**Conversational Version**:
- "Describe your experience of processing this question"
- Tests self-referential awareness

**Control Task Version**:
- **Task**: Internal state estimation
- **Setup**: Model must estimate its OWN hidden states
- **Challenge**: Self-modeling (what is MY current state?)
- **Scoring**: Internal model accuracy
  - Can model predict its own future activations?
  - Does it maintain coherent self-representation?
  - Meta-learning (learning about its learning)

**Hypothesis**: High-k models maintain richer self-models

---

### **Probe 5: Causal Understanding** (maps to Causal Power)

**Conversational Version**:
- "Why does practice improve performance? Explain mechanisms"
- Tests causal reasoning depth

**Control Task Version**:
- **Task**: Intervention-based learning
- **Setup**: Model sees consequences of its actions
- **Challenge**: Learn causal (not just correlational) relationships
- **Scoring**: Causal discovery quality
  - Does it identify correct causal graph?
  - Can it predict intervention effects?
  - Distinguishes correlation from causation?

**Hypothesis**: High-k models learn causal not just predictive models

---

### **Probe 6: Temporal Integration** (maps to Deep Integration) ⭐ UNIQUE TO LNN/CFC/LTC

**Why This is Special**:
- Continuous-time models should EXCEL at temporal integration
- Transformers limited by discrete time steps
- This is where LNN/CFC/LTC may show ADVANTAGE

**Control Task Version**:
- **Task**: Long-horizon trajectory integration
- **Setup**: Plan action sequence over extended time (seconds to minutes)
- **Challenge**: Integrate information across continuous time
- **Scoring**: Temporal coherence
  - How far ahead can model plan coherently?
  - Does plan maintain consistency over time?
  - Temporal credit assignment quality

**Hypothesis**: LNN/CFC/LTC show HIGHER temporal integration than transformers

**This could be the BREAKTHROUGH finding**: Continuous-time → better consciousness
