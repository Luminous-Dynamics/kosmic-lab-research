### Critical Success Factors
1. **Vision-proxy gap transparency**: This is our key philosophical innovation—ensure it's emphasized throughout
2. **Validation strength**: Highlight r=0.98 GDP correlation and p<10^-149 significance
3. **Policy relevance**: Emphasize SDG monitoring, early warning, climate governance applications
4. **Research program framing**: Position as Paper 1 of 3, with clear trajectory toward actualization measurement

### Common Submission Mistakes to Avoid
- ❌ Missing co-author approval emails
- ❌ Forgetting to upload supplementary materials
- ❌ Not checking figure resolution (blurry figures = desk reject)
- ❌ Exceeding word limit (automatic desk reject at many journals)
- ❌ Generic cover letter (shows lack of attention)
- ❌ Not suggesting reviewers (editors appreciate guidance)
- ❌ Submitting on Friday afternoon (gets lost in weekend pile)

### Optimal Submission Timing
- ✅ **Best days**: Tuesday-Thursday mornings
- ✅ **Best months**: September-November, January-March (avoid summer, December, April)
- ✅ **Avoid**: Right before major conferences (editors swamped)
