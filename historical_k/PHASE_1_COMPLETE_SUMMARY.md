**Summary**: We measure **material foundations** (infrastructure) not **consciousness qualities** (essence).

- **H2 (Interconnectedness)**: Financial flows ≠ empathic resonance (**very large gap**)
- **H6 (Pan-Sentient)**: Human health ≠ care for all beings (**very large gap**)
- **H7 (Evolution)**: Technology ≠ wise consciousness (**large gap**)
- **H1 (Coherence)**: Communication ≠ harmonious integration (**large gap**)
- **H4 (Play)**: Economic complexity ≠ joyful play (**large gap**)

**Moderate Gaps** (proxies capture some essence):

- **H5 (Wisdom)**: Education enables wisdom transmission (**moderate gap**)

**Framing**: "We measure the soil, not the flower"—material prerequisites for consciousness evolution, not consciousness itself.

```
Each harmony represents a consciousness quality (see Table: Vision-Proxy

consciousness-direct measures are unavailable historically. Future papers
will progressively close this gap using contemporary surveys, ethnography,
and experiential data (Papers 2-3).
```

```
Our proxies systematically underestimate consciousness qualities:

Despite these limitations, our findings reveal meaningful patterns: 13.12x
growth in material foundations (1810-2020), with post-war acceleration and
```
